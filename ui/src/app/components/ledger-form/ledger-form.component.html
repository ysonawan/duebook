How to Create Ledger Entries

  • Debit (बाकी): Customer owes you money. Increases their balance.
  • Credit (जमा): Customer pays you. Decreases their balance.
  • Balance (निव्वळ): Automatically calculated based on entry type and amount.
  • Notes: Use this to document references, invoice numbers, or reasons for the entry.
